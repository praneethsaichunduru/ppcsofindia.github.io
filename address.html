ADDRESSING THE ENVELOPES

Rank of a Postmaster varies for each post office. Here are the ranks of Postmaster mentioned in the table below:

POST OFFICE ACRONYM/RANK POST OFFICE FULL FORM POSTMASTER LEVEL
GPO GENERAL POST OFFICE SENIOR POSTMASTER
HO HEAD OFFICE
PB PHILATELIC BUREAU DIRECTOR or INCHARGE
SO SUB OFFICE SUB POSTMASTER
BO BRANCH OFFICE BRANCH POSTMASTER

Based on the above ranks, we must fill the address on the envelope which we are sending. Fortunately, unlike an individual's address (wwhich involves details such as street name, phone number, district, etc...), we simply can write a post office's address as shown below:

FORMAT EXAMPLE
______ Postmaster,
{Post Office and Rank},
{Name of the town/city},
{District (dist.)},
{State} - {Pincode}. 		Sub Postmaster,
Shaniwar Wada SO,
Pune,
Pune (dist.),
Maharashtra - 411030.
