HOW TO SEND AN SASE

Self Addressed and Stamped Envelope (SASE) is an envelope which contains the items that should be returned to us, along with an envelope with our address written and appropriate amount of stamps affixed to it. It is considered one of the efficient methods to get a PPC from a post office.

  Preparation

1. Prepare the postal stationery (postcards, printed postcards & covers - with apropriate stamps, inland letter cards) to be sent to the designated post office.

2. Two covers are required, one for sending to the destination post office and another for receiving. Make sure to choose the covers large enough for the stationery to fit in when sending and receiving.

3. As per current norms, for each 20 grams (in ordinary post), INR 5 worth stamps must be affixed on the cover. Minimum charge for the registered post is INR 22.

4. If sending via registered post, make sure to mention REGD POST on the cover.

Note:Make sure to check with the parcel(s) weight at the post office, so it can come bac to you properly.

  Writing your Address

1. Receiver's address should be written on the left section of the front side of the cover.

2. Sender's address should be written on the back side of the cover.

3. Appropriate amount of stamps should be affixed on the right section of the front side of the cover.
